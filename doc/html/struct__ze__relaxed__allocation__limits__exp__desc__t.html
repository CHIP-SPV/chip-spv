Detailed Description

Relaxed limits memory allocation descriptor.

  • This structure may be passed to ::zeMemAllocShared or ::zeMemAllocDevice, via pNext member of ::ze_device_mem_alloc_desc_t.
  • This structure may also be passed to ::zeMemAllocHost, via pNext member of ::ze_host_mem_alloc_desc_t.
